## What is GTO Drill?

GTO Drill is a crowd-powered poker training app. Sign in, play solver-verified scenarios, rate your confidence, and learn alongside a crowd — see how your reads stack up against the field and against optimal play.

- **45-scenario library** covering preflop through river
- **Confidence ratings** that surface high-confidence disagreements
- **Crowd breakdowns** showing how everyone answered
- **Villain ranges & equity tools** with an interactive hand matrix
- **Mobile-first** with 44px tap targets and offline support
