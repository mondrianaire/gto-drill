Each panel is a working ~375px-wide mockup. Tap around to feel each workflow. Selection state is local to the panel.

Villain range picker — 13 × 13

Current (Pass 1): horizontal scroll. Pinpoint precision but small targets and you have to scroll to see all 13 columns.
Alternative — Master-detail: tap a rank on the left, the relevant hands appear on the right. Always-visible big buttons; no scrolling required. Two taps per hand class instead of one.

Hero / board card picker — 4 × 13

Current (Pass 1): horizontal scroll over a 4-suit × 13-rank card grid. Cards are recognizable but you have to scroll right to reach low ranks.
Alternative — Rank → suit two-step: tap a rank, then tap a suit. Big targets at every step; selecting one card is two taps but each one is fat-finger-proof.
